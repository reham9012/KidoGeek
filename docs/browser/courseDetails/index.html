About Us

KidoGeek is the leading online coding school for kids and teens ages 8-18. Our mission is to prepare students for success in the digital world by teaching them how to code.

What We Do

KidoGeek is a woman and minority-owned business whose mission is to empower the next generation of coders and entrepreneurs to use their skills to make the world a better place.

Overview

Course Content

CodaKid Web Development delves into the foundations of web development using HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Projects in this track involve the ground up creation of your own custom website and understanding how to use browser tools to create them.

Upon successful completion of Web Development, CodaKid students will be ready to graduate to other CodaKid Core or Elective tracks.
